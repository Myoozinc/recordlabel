01

Who We Are

MYOOZ InC ("we", "us", "our") is a music record label operating at myoozinc.github.io/recordlabel. We represent artists, offer music production and label services, and sell official merchandise.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, submit a contact form, or place a merchandise or service order.

Data controller contact:
MYOOZ InC · info@myoozinc.com
02

Data We Collect

We only collect information that is necessary to provide our services.

Contact Form
Name, email address, and any message content you voluntarily submit.
Merchandise Orders
Name, email address, shipping address, and order details. Payment is processed externally — we do not store payment card information.
Service Inquiries
Name, email, and project details you share when requesting label or production services.
Technical / Browsing Data
IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time of visit — collected automatically by hosting infrastructure (GitHub Pages). We may also use analytics tools for aggregate traffic insights.
03

How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your contact form submissions and inquiries
  • To process and fulfill merchandise orders, including shipping coordination
  • To communicate updates about your order or service request
  • To improve website performance and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

04

Who We Share Your Data With

We may share your data only with trusted third parties who assist us in operating the site and fulfilling orders:

  • Shipping/logistics providers — to deliver merchandise (name + address only)
  • Payment processors — when applicable, for secure payment handling
  • Email service providers — to send order confirmations and replies
  • GitHub Pages (hosting) — our hosting provider may collect basic access logs

All third parties are contractually obligated to handle your data securely and only for the purposes we specify.

05

Cookies & Tracking

Our site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic functionality and analytics. These may include:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function properly
  • Analytics cookies — to understand how visitors use the site (e.g., page views, session duration)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect site functionality.

06

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary:

  • Contact form messages: up to 12 months after the last communication
  • Order records: up to 5 years for accounting and legal compliance
  • Analytics/technical data: typically aggregated and anonymized within 90 days

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Your Rights below).

07

Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing
  • Opt-out — of any marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@myoozinc.com. We will respond within 30 days.

08

GDPR — EU Users

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies. Our legal bases for processing are:

  • Consent — for contact form submissions and optional communications
  • Contract — to fulfill merchandise orders and service agreements
  • Legitimate interest — for basic analytics and site security
  • Legal obligation — to comply with applicable law

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (DPA) if you believe your rights have been violated.

09

CCPA — California Users

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grants you additional rights:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • The right to delete your personal information
  • The right to opt-out of the sale of personal information — we do not sell personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To submit a CCPA request, email us at info@myoozinc.com.

10

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.
11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you via the contact information we hold on file.
